Beneath the Clouds, Beyond the River

Welcome to a land of mystery and danger. A sprawling canyon, carved over millennia by the roaring river, lies at the heart of an untouched, ancient, and alive world knows as Ashroot. The jagged cliffs rise high above, narrowing as the river twists and turns, a lifeline for the brave who dare venture down. Below, the dark caves hum with untold secrets, rumored to be filled with ancient relics and precious minerals. But beware—many who’ve sought riches here never returned.

Explore, Mine, Survive the Rapids

In the wild heart of Ashroot, survival depends on both your wits and your skills. The journey begins with the river, a force of nature that offers both challenges and opportunities. Rafting through the canyon’s treacherous waters will test your reflexes and endurance. Navigate sharp rocks, whirlpools, and cascading waterfalls while searching for supplies and avoiding dangerous wildlife.

One Soul Against the Stone Giant

Who are you? A lone adventurer seeking fame and fortune, or a desperate soul trying to survive in a world that doesn’t care? Your past is a mystery, your motivations unclear, but one thing is certain—you’re here, in the canyon, searching for something. What that is, only you know.

The Summit No One Ever Returned From

At the heart of Ashroot lies the summit—an impenetrable peak hidden behind swirling clouds. No one who has ventured to the top has ever returned, and the stories that circulate about it are enough to keep most people at a safe distance. Some say it’s a place of unimaginable wealth, while others believe it holds a force so ancient, it’s better left undisturbed.
